[2009.10.02] Mandelbrot Applet

by Tamar van Steenbergen, student no. 3233308
University of Utrecht, Department English Language & Culture


The four applets below show details of a Mandelbrot set.
The text fields in the applets respectively show:

  • the x-coordinate of the centre of the Mandelbrot space;
  • the y-coordinate of the centre of the Mandelbrot space;
  • the maximum number of iterations;
  • the scale factor;
  • the zoom factor: multiplication factor for the scale factor at each click.
To zoom in, click the left mouse button.
To zoom out, either click the right mouse button or press control while clicking the left.

To select a colour theme, select a theme from the drop-down menu.

To set the variables to default, click the reset button. The colour theme will not change.
Enjoy :)
