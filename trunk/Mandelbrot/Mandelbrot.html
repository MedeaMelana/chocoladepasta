Mandelbrot Applet

by Tamar van Steenbergen, student no. 3233308
The four applets below are show details of a Mandelbrot set.

The textfields in the applets respectively show:

  • the x coordinate of the center of the Mandelspace;
  • the y coordinate of the center of the Mandelspace;
  • the maximum number of iterations;
  • the scale factor;
  • the zoom factor.
You can choose a color theme by selecting one of the options in
the drop-down menu, and by clicking the Reset-button, the applet
zooms out completely. The color theme, however, remains the same.

Right click to zoom in.
Left click or ctrl + right click to zoom out.
