Mandelbrot Applet

by Tamar van Steenbergen, student no. 3233308
The four applets below show details of a Mandelbrot set.

The textfields in the applets respectively show:

  • the x-coordinate of the center of the Mandelbrot space;
  • the y-coordinate of the center of the Mandelbrot space;
  • the maximum number of iterations;
  • the scale factor;
  • the zoom factor.
You can choose a color theme by selecting one of the options in
the drop-down menu. By clicking the Reset-button, the applet
zooms out completely. The color theme, however, remains the same.

Left click to zoom in.
Right click or ctrl + left click to zoom out.
Enjoy :)
